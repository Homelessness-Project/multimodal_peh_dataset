This proposed legislation is similar to other successful state redistrict ing plans across the nation and it seeks to establish 100 member independent citizen commission . The commission will be independent . No current or recent elected official , candidates , political party officials or their aids or spouses would be eligible . Any PA registered voter that meets independent criteria can apply to be part of the commission . The commission will be transparent . It will establish transparent procedures , follow the strict timetable and provide meaningful opportunity for the public input prior to withdrawing the plans and, again, before adopting the final plans. All information will be available on a publically accessible website and the commission will be impartial and ethically bound.
